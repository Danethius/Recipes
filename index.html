Pork tenderloin is a underappreciated cut of meat. It is one of the finest cuts of pork. It is both tender and lean like a filet mingion and typically very cheap at the grocery store vs. a beef tenderloin. This website has 3 different recipes for cooking this fantastic cut of meat.
