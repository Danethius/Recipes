Balsamic Rosemary Pork Tenderloin

Description

This savory and slightly sweet version of the pork tenderloin is delightful. As with the other recipes we will marinade it for at least 30 minutes, up to 24 hours. To make the marinade you simply stir together the marinade ingredients then pour the marinade over the pork tenderloin in a bowl or large ziplock bag.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chopped rosemary (you can use dried rosemary in a pinch)
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 1 ½ teaspoons garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
